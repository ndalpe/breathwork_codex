# Voice Kit

This version supports pre-recorded voice clips.

## Desired voice direction

Calm, feminine, happy, enthusiastic, soft, meditative, reassuring.

Think:
- warm smile in the voice
- gentle pace
- not theatrical
- not robotic
- relaxed but alive
- clear enough to understand during breathwork

## Suggested recording style

Record each clip with about 0.2 seconds of silence at the start and 0.3 seconds at the end.

For numbers:
- Keep them short and clean.
- Smile slightly while recording.
- Use a soft but clear tone.

For phase cues:
- "Inhale" should feel inviting and upward.
- "Hold" should feel stable and reassuring.
- "Exhale" should feel relaxing and downward.

## AI voice prompt

Use this prompt in a voice-generation tool you have permission to use:

Calm feminine meditation guide voice. Warm, happy, gently enthusiastic, peaceful, clear, soft, slow but not sleepy. Natural human delivery. Minimal breath noise. No music. No background sound. Record each word or number as a separate short audio clip.

## Recommended export settings

- Format: MP3
- Bitrate: 128 kbps or higher
- Sample rate: 44.1 kHz
- Normalize loudness consistently across all clips
