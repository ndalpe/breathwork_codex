# Motion

## Breathing Guidance

- Motion should support the breathing rhythm without distracting from it.
- The breathing state must remain understandable without relying only on motion.

## Transitions

- Use simple transitions for opening menus, expanding panels, and entering or leaving session states.
- Avoid excessive animation during active breathwork.

## Reduced Motion

- Respect the user's reduced-motion preference.
- Provide a calmer or static alternative for breathing coach animation when reduced motion is enabled.

## Feedback

- Voice and haptic feedback are user-controlled from the header.
- Haptic feedback must not be required to understand the session state.
- Audio guidance must not be required to understand the session state.
